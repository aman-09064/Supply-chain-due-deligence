#  Supplier Code of Conduct  
### Ethical, Responsible & Sustainable Business Expectations  
Aligned with: OECD Guidelines • UNGP • ILO • CSRD • EU ESG Standards

This Supplier Code of Conduct outlines minimum requirements all suppliers must follow to work with our organization.

---

# 1 Human Rights & Labor Standards
Suppliers must:
- Prohibit forced, bonded, or child labor  
- Ensure safe working conditions  
- Provide fair wages and working hours  
- Respect worker freedom of association  
- Prevent discrimination  

ILO Core Conventions must be upheld.

---

# 2 Environmental Responsibility
Suppliers must:
- Manage waste, emissions & chemicals responsibly  
- Reduce environmental footprint  
- Comply with environmental laws  
- Avoid environmental harm to communities  
- Track energy, water, and GHG emissions where possible  

Alignment with **ISO 14001**, **CSRD**, **ESRS E1/E2/E3** recommended.

---

# 3 Business Ethics & Anti-Corruption
Suppliers must:
- Maintain zero-tolerance for bribery and fraud  
- Prohibit facilitation payments  
- Implement conflict-of-interest policies  
- Maintain transparent financial records  
- Provide anti-corruption training  

Applicable laws: UK Bribery Act, FCPA, EU Anti-Corruption Directive.

---

# 4 Health & Safety
Suppliers must:
- Provide safe equipment  
- Train workers in safety practices  
- Prevent accidents  
- Maintain emergency preparedness  

Follow **ISO 45001** and local safety laws.

---

# 5 Data Security & Privacy
Suppliers must:
- Protect personal data under GDPR / local laws  
- Implement access controls & encryption  
- Notify of data breaches promptly  

---

# 6 Supply Chain Transparency
Suppliers must:
- Map and monitor their own upstream suppliers  
- Ensure sub-suppliers meet similar ESG expectations  
- Conduct risk assessments  
- Avoid high-risk sourcing unless mitigated  

---

# 7 Monitoring & Audits
The company reserves the right to:
- Conduct audits  
- Request documentation  
- Verify compliance  
- Require corrective actions  

Non-compliance may result in termination.

---

By signing, the supplier agrees to uphold this Code of Conduct.
